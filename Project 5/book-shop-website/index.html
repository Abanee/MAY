Two Ways to Read

Book Shop & Lending Library

Marlowe & Finch has always been two things at once — a curated bookshop where you take a story home for keeps, and a lending library where you borrow your way through thousands of titles. Two paths. One address.

The Book Shop

Own a Story

Browse, Buy & Collect

Our ground-floor shop is stocked with carefully chosen new releases, classic editions, local authors, and rare collector's finds. Every title earns its place. Browse with a cup of coffee. Leave with something you'll keep forever.

  • ✦ Curated new arrivals & bestsellers
  • ✦ Local & independent author titles
  • ✦ Collector & leather-bound editions
  • ✦ In-store coffee nook & reading chairs
  • ✦ Complimentary wax-seal gift wrapping
Explore the Bookshop →
The Library

Borrow a World

Borrow, Track & Return

Upstairs, the lending library holds over 18,400 titles across 14 genres. Members borrow books, track return deadlines from a personal dashboard, drop returns in our 24-hour box, and get gentle reminders before a due date. No surprises. Just reading.

  • ✦ 18,400+ titles across 14 genres
  • ✦ Digital borrow & return deadline tracker
  • ✦ Priority holds on new arrivals
  • ✦ 24-hour drop-box returns & renewals
  • ✦ Members-only literary events
