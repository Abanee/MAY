How It Works

The Reading Journey

From selecting your first title to earning reader privileges — here is how lending works at Marlowe & Finch.

1
Step 01 · Explore

Choose Your Books

Wander our physical oak aisles or search 18,400+ titles online by mood, genre, or reading duration.

2
Step 02 · Join

Become a Member

Select your membership tier in under 2 minutes — receive your digital library card instantly.

3
Step 03 · Checkout

Borrow & Collect

Scan your card at the counter for instant checkout, or request same-day doorstep delivery.

4
Step 04 · Experience

Read & Relax

Take your time — members enjoy access to quiet fireside reading rooms long after the main shop closes.

5
Step 05 · Easy Drop

Return Anytime

Drop finished books in our 24/7 exterior chute, or tap once in the app to renew your borrow period.

6
Step 06 · Privileges

Earn Reader Rewards

Every return earns points toward author readings, artisan merchandise, and rare vault access.
