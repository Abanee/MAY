— MISSION

The Aggregator
That Serves Craft.

ArtisanGrid is a multi-service platform aggregating local handymen across trades — distinct from a single-trade contractor's own website. We connect homeowners with the full spectrum of skilled tradespeople in their area: plumbers, electricians, carpenters, HVAC technicians, painters, and general repairers.

Unlike booking platforms that take 15–30% commission, we generate revenue through optional artisan memberships — not cuts of your earnings. The artisan keeps every dollar the customer pays them.

Our technology does the matchmaking. The artisan does the work. The customer gets transparency. Nobody gets exploited.

— TRUST & SAFETY

How We Keep
Everyone Safe.

Every artisan on ArtisanGrid is put through a rigorous vetting process before they can take a single booking.

🔍

Background Checks

Every artisan undergoes a full criminal record check, identity verification, and address confirmation before joining the network.

📜

License Verification

Trade licenses are verified directly with issuing authorities. Electricians, plumbers, and HVAC techs must hold current certifications relevant to their sector.

🛡️

Insurance Coverage

All artisans must carry a minimum £1M / $1M public liability insurance. Proof is validated annually and displayed on every profile.

⭐

Neighbor Reviews

Only verified customers who completed a job can leave a review. No fake 5-stars. Artisans below 4.0 are flagged for review by our quality team.

💳

Secure Payments

Payments are held in escrow until you confirm job completion. Dispute resolution within 48 hours. Full refunds processed for unresolved issues.

📞

24/7 Support

Our safety team is available around the clock. In an emergency, call our direct line — we'll escalate immediately and coordinate the nearest available specialist.

— HISTORY

How We
Got Here.

2021

Founded in Sector 4

After a burst pipe and three unreliable contractors, co-founders Mia and Dev built ArtisanGrid over a weekend. First 12 artisans were friends and neighbors.

2022

First 1,000 Jobs

Network expanded to 45 artisans across 4 trades. First emergency 24/7 coverage launched. Zero commission policy written into the founding charter.

2023

Dashboard Launch

Real-time artisan tracking dashboard launched. Live ETA, job status, and neighbor reviews all in one place. 80 artisans, 8 trade categories.

2024

Multi-Sector Expansion

Expanded to 14 sectors. Background check program formalized with third-party verification partners. Pro membership tier launched.

2026

Today: 148 Artisans, 18 Trades

4,820 jobs completed. Average platform rating of 4.84 stars. Still zero commission. Still founded on the belief that craft deserves respect.
