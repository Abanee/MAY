— QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked
Questions.

Clear answers on how the network operates.

How does the emergency 24/7 dispatch work? ▼
Our dispatch system monitors active verified artisans in your sector. If you flag a request as an emergency, our radar matches the nearest active expert. They receive an urgent notification on their dashboard and accept the dispatch within minutes.
Are the price estimates guaranteed? ▼
Estimates are generated from aggregate historical data in your sector. The actual quote is agreed directly with the artisan before work begins—no hidden platform adjustments.
How are artisans vetted on the network? ▼
Every member undergoes a background check, sector license verification, and reference validation. Ratings come exclusively from homeowners with verified job history on the platform.
How does the platform fund itself if commission is 0%? ▼
Artisans can optionally subscribe to a Pro membership ($19/mo) for premium dashboard metrics, route optimization, and enhanced list visibility. Listing is always free, and commission is always 0%.
