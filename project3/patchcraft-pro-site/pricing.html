COST FACTORS

What Determines Your Custom Patch Cost?

Stitch Coverage

Price factors in embroidery percentage. Starter quotes cover up to 75% coverage. 100% full-stitch fills take longer on looms.

Backing Options

Standard sew-on is baseline. Specialty backings like Velcro loops/hooks or heavy industrial iron-on adhesives add +$0.15 to +$0.30 per unit.

Borders & Shapes

Classic circles and shields use merrowed stitches (standard cost). Intricate shapes require laser hot-cut borders (+$0.10 per unit).

Specialty Materials

Metallic threads, glow-in-the-dark yarn, reflective backdrops, or double-thick PVC molds have fixed tooling setups.
