BADGE DESIGN GUIDE

What Makes a Great School Badge?

Our design consultants have produced badges for hundreds of institutions. Here are the key factors we recommend for long-lasting, visually striking school patches.

Limit to 5 Thread Colours
More than 5 colours increases stitch complexity. We match your Pantone or RAL references exactly.
Minimum 2.5" for Detail Work
Fine lettering and crests need at least 2.5" diameter to be legible. We'll advise on the right size.
Choose the Right Backing
Iron-on for blazers, sew-on for sports kits, velcro for interchangeable house systems. We guide you through the options.
Archive for Easy Reorders
We archive every stitch file and colour profile. Next year's reorder takes under 1 hour to process.
