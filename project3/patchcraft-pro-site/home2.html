Our standard MOQ is 50 units for embroidered and woven patches, and 75 units for PVC rubber badges. Woven labels have an MOQ of 100 units. For corporate accounts placing recurring orders, we can discuss lower MOQs on a case-by-case basis.

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, SVG, PSD, and PNG files. Vector formats at 300 DPI are preferred. If you only have a low-res file, our in-house digitisers will recreate it at no extra charge for orders over 250 units.
