The PopCraft Method

The Craft Behind
The Perfect Crunch.

We don't believe in shortcuts. From sourcing rare, heritage grain kernels to hand-tossing spices, our three-step method guarantees a popping experience unlike any other.

01

Heritage Heirloom Grains

We partner with multi-generational family farms to source premium, non-GMO heirloom kernels. These ancient grains pop into fluffy, structurally robust shapes that hold toppings beautifully.

02

Controlled Batch Popping

Instead of drowning kernels in heavy commercial oils, we pop in custom-engineered kettles using organic coconut oil. Meticulous temperature controls ensure every pop is perfectly crisp.

03

Hand-Tossed Gourmet Spices

While the popcorn is warm, we tumble and toss it by hand with artisanal seasonings — from French fleur de sel to aged Parmigiano-Reggiano. Even coating, zero compromise.
