Black Truffles

Périgord black truffles hand-foraged in France, shaved and freeze-dried to lock in maximum earthy, complex aroma.

Origin: France

Grass-Fed Ghee

Pasteur-raised cows' butter clarified slowly over open fires in small batches, yielding an incredibly rich and nutty dairy profile.

Delicate sea salt crystals hand-harvested from medieval marshes in Brittany. A soft crunch with clean mineral sweetness.

Origin: Brittany, FR

Uji Matcha

Origin: Kyoto, JP
