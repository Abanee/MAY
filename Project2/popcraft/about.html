Our Heritage & Craft

Where Gourmet Meets The Humble Kernel

Handcrafted in small batches since 2019, PopCraft reimagines the snacking experience by combining rare heirloom grains with globally‑sourced, all‑natural spices.

The Founder's Journey

It Started With A Single Copper Kettle in Brooklyn

In 2019, founder Abigail Sterling grew tired of chemical‑heavy microwave bags and dry, flavorless theater popcorn. She set out with a simple mission: elevate the humble corn kernel into a sophisticated culinary canvas.

Working in a small garage workspace, she experimented with dozens of botanical infusions, gourmet spices, and popping oils. By replacing industrial vegetable oils with cold‑pressed organic coconut oil and dry-air popping techniques, she unlocked a cleaner, crisper popcorn texture.

Today, our kitchen has grown, but we stay true to Abigail's original rules: small batches, direct-trade organic grains, and hand-tossed spices. We never rely on artificial flavoring, colors, or preservatives.

ESTABLISHED 2019

Brooklyn Kitchen Beginnings

How We Pop

Our Three Unwavering Craft Standards

01

Heritage Heirloom Grains

We source single-origin organic kernels from multi-generational family farms in Indiana and Nebraska. These heirloom grains yield thin, tender hulls that pop into massive, fluffy shapes and prevent shrapnel from sticking in your teeth.

02

Controlled Air-Popping

Instead of frying kernels in heavy grease, we pop them using precision-controlled hot air at exactly 460°F. This creates a clean, dry, and super-crisp canvas that holds spices without turning soggy.

03

Hand-Tossed Infusions

While the popped kernels are warm, we tumble them in small copper bowls, hand-drizzling pure grass-fed ghee or botanical syrups, and dusting proprietary dry spice rubs for uniform flavor distribution.

Seed to Kernel

Ethical Sourcing, Pure Ingredients

We travel the globe and partner with single-source cooperatives to ensure every ingredient in PopCraft meets our rigorous standards.

01. Heritage Kernels

Indiana Family Farms

We grow our organic non-GMO "Ruby Red" and "Mushroom" grains on dedicated family acreage in Indiana. Our specific grain choices produce thick, round kernels that pop cleanly and minimize sharp debris.

Direct Trade Certified
02. Clean Base

Cold-Pressed Oils

Rather than frying or coating in high-heat canola or palm oils, we use 100% organic, cold-pressed virgin coconut oil harvested in Southern Luzon. This ensures a clean, buttery flavor note and zero trans fats.

100% Organic & Non-GMO
03. Global Seasonings

Single-Origin Spices

We curate our spices directly from global cooperatives—from organic Tellicherry pepper from Kerala, India, to real Fleur de Sel harvested from ancient French salt marshes. No artificial flavor dust, ever.

Direct Sourced From Farms

Our Journey

Evolution of The Pop

2019

The Spark & The Kettle

Abigail Sterling pops the first batch of Sweet & Salty Caramel in a 10-gallon copper kettle in a small Brooklyn garage. We began selling locally at neighborhood greenmarkets.

2021

First Artisan Kitchen

PopCraft establishes its brick-and-mortar storefront and commercial popping room in Brooklyn. Customers are lured in by the intoxicating aroma of roasting pecans and bubbling brown sugar.

2024

Nationwide Shipping

We launch the monthly PopCraft subscription club. Handcrafted signature tins begin arriving fresh on doorsteps nationwide, maintaining our small-batch standards at scale.

2026

Sustainable Frontiers

Our roasting kitchen transitions to 100% solar power and we pilot fully compostable plant-based bags for our kernel catalog, paving the way for low-impact gourmet snacking.
