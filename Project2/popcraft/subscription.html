FAQ

Common Questions

If you subscribe before the 1st of the month, your first box ships on the 5th. Subscribe after the 1st, and your first box ships the following month's 5th. You'll receive a shipping confirmation with tracking as soon as your box is on its way.

Classic and Taster boxes are curated by our flavor team — that's part of the discovery experience! Connoisseur members can set flavor preferences (sweet, savory, spicy, etc.) in their member portal, and we'll customize accordingly while still including at least one surprise.

Simply log into your member portal and select "Skip Month" or "Pause Subscription" before the 28th of the current month. There's no fee or penalty — you can skip up to 3 months per year with no questions asked.

We want you to love every single bag. If something doesn't hit the mark, reach out within 14 days and we'll send you a replacement flavor or credit your account. Your feedback also helps us improve our curation!

Currently we ship to the US, Canada, UK, and Australia. International subscriptions ship on the 7th of each month. A small international shipping surcharge applies — we're working on bringing costs down as we grow our international warehousing.

Absolutely! Our gift subscriptions are available in 3, 6, and 12 month increments. The recipient gets a beautiful gift card in the mail and can activate their box on their schedule. Check out our Gift Tins page for more gifting options.
