Paint Defect Analyzer

Select a common paint anomaly below to analyze how AURA technicians reconstruct the clear coat to restore perfect optical reflection.

Defect Profile: Moderate Surface Scratches

Swirl Marks & Spiderweb Scratching

Created by improper hand washing, mechanical brushes, or rough wiping. These micro-scratches act as tiny concave mirrors, scattering light rays in multiple directions instead of reflecting them parallel, destroying gloss depth.

AURA Correction Action: A two-stage paint correction. First, rotary heavy compounding cuts down clear coat surrounding scratches to level surface. Second, dual-action jewel polish refines paint haze to deliver a mirror finish.
