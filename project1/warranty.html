Maintenance Rules

Recommended Aftercare Guide

To ensure your warranty remains valid and your hydrophobic finish continues to deflect water properly, follow our approved wash protocols:

Approved Wash Method

Wash your vehicle every 2 to 3 weeks using a pH-neutral snow foam cannon and the two-bucket hand wash method with plush microfibers. Avoid automatic brush washes.

Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Do not apply waxes, sealants, or abrasive polishing compounds over your ceramic coating or PPF. Refrain from washing in direct sunlight to avoid mineral spot etching.

Annual Inspections

Lifetime Ceramic Pro coverage requires an annual inspection and booster top-up to verify surface health and maintain active registration in our global warranty system.
